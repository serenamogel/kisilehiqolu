Raichu (Japanese: ライチュウ Raichu) is a Lightning-type Stage 1 Pokémon card. It is part of the Expedition Base Set.

e-Reader data

The card ID of the Holofoil print is B-82. The card ID of the Non Holofoil print is B-82-#. On the Non Holofoil print, the short strip contains Pokédex information and a brief card summary. There is no long strip for this card. The species, height, and weight are present on the Holofoil print.
