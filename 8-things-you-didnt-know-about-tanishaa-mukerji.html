➡ Tanishaa Mukerji Biography

An Indian actress, Tanisha Mukherji, better known as Kajol’s younger sister. She has been active in Hindi cinema for more than a decade but has not done anything special that adds her to the list of most popular and successful actresses. On the other hand, her elder sister, Kajol has done a lot in her career life which makes her stand out. Be it Kajol’s expressions or acting prowess, she is a champ. Despite that, Kajol never helped Tanisha to get fame or to shape up her career. Well, here we are going to share something more about Tanisha Mukherji that you probably didn’t know before. Let’s have a look!  

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Tanishaa Mukerji

1- Relatives In Bollywood

Tanisha Mukherji’s cousins are associated with the Bollywood film industry. Here you will be surprised to know that Rani Mukherji, Sharbani Mukherji, Mohnish Behl, and director Ayan Mukherji are her cousins.

2- Famous For

We all think that Tanisha is new to the showbiz field, but let us tell you that she first started off her career as a VJ in 1995 for Channel V. It was the time when she used to anchor the most popular show named BPL Oye.

3- Acted Alongside Hotshots

It is fact that Tanisha Mukherji’s acting talent couldn’t bring any fame or establish her name in the industry, but she has acted alongside many Bollywood hotshots, like; Suniel Shetty, Vidya Malvade, Dino Morea, and many more.

4- Multi-lingual

Tanisha Mukherji is multi-lingual as she is fluent in Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Marathi, and Telugu languages as well. And this led her to get many acting gigs in not just Bollywood, yet South Indian movies as well.

5- Earned a Nomination

Tanisha Mukherji has a credit associated with her acting career. In 2007, she earned a nomination for the Vijay Award for ‘Best Debut Actress’ for a Tamil movie named ‘Unnale Unnale’, 

6- Popular Movie

Throughout her acting career, there is only one film that helped Tanisha make a mark was none other than ‘Neal And Nikki’ wherein she was seen alongside Uday Chopra.

7- Came Under Spotlight

Tanisha Mukherji came under the spotlight after filming several bold scenes with Uday Chopra in their film named ‘Neal And Nikki’. But still, her transformation and hype couldn’t give her any success in her career.

8- Rumors

After working on the screen with Uday Chopra, there were rumors speculated that the on-screen couple dated each other for more than two years, but later, they parted their ways.
