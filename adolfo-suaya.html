Biography

Adolfo Suaya was born in Argentina on March 16, 1961. Argentine-American restaurateur, hotelier, actor, and former TV host of the Sun Channel shows Latin American Foodie and Foodie Trips. He owns several restaurants across Southern California, and hotels in Uruguay and Argentina. He was a longtime friend of writer Jonathan Gold, and posted a tribute on his Instagram when Jonathan passed away in 1988. He dropped out of high school in order to pursue a career in entrepreneurship. He started his first business at 17 – a children’s clothing company – and his second at age 19, a ski jacket design and manufacture business. He moved to Los Angeles in 1984 to study acting at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute.
