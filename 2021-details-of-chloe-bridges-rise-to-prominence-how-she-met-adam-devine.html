Details of Chloe Bridges Rise To Prominence and How She Met Adam DeVine

January 19, 2024
blog

The American actress Chloe Bridges isn’t just amazing; she’s also the definition of ‘beauty with brains’. The charming movie star has garnered fame through her brilliant and exceptional roles in movies and TV series. She is notable for her appearance in the 2005 ABC comedy series Freddie. Chloe has equally appeared on other popular TV shows including Pretty Little Liars and The Carrie Diaries, just to mention a few. Since the inception of her career in 2005, Chloe’s gorgeous looks and acting skills have made her a fan favorite. In addition to her acting prowess, she has been in the limelight following her relationship with the American comedian Adam DeVine.

Learn more about Chloe Bridges bio’ and other interesting facts below.

Chloe Bridges life before the fame

The actress was born on the 27th day of December 1991 in the Thibodaux neighborhood of Louisiana in the United States. Her birth name is Chloe Suazo but she later adopted Chloe Bridges as her professional name. She grew up along with her parents who are both of British and Honduran descent. Her nationality is American while she is of mixed ethnic heritage.

Growing up, Chloe spent her early years in Southern California where she graduated from John Muir Middle School. She developed a passion for acting when she was very young and she appeared in several plays and dramas organized by her school. The actress also got her bachelor’s degree from Columbia University.

How she became famous

Starting her acting career in 2005, Chloe won her first onscreen role in the ABC comedy series Freddie. Her excellent performance as Zoey Moreno quickly brought her into the limelight and also helped her get bigger roles later on. She followed it up with an appearance in George Lopez in 2006. She took her acting to the big screen by making an appearance in the 2007 movie The Longshots alongside Ice Cube and Keke Palmer. After that, she starred in Legally Blondes where she played Ashley Meadows along with Lisa Banes in 2009.

In 2010, she featured in the Disney Channel series Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam, playing the role of Dana Turner alongside Demi Lovato and the Jonas Brothers. Her next big project was The Carrie Diaries where she played a recurring role of Donna from 2013 to 2014. Afterward, she joined the cast of Pretty Little Liars where she starred as Sydney Driscoll from 2014 to 2017. Her other notable projects include Faking It, The Grinder, Daytime Divas, Insatiable, Little Bitches, Game Over, Man! among others.

How did she meet Adam DeVine?

Chloe Bridges has won the hearts of her many fans with her wonderful acting style; she is also successful in her love life. The actress has been in a relationship with the American actor, Adam DeVine. Adam is a multi-talented American actor and comedian who garnered fame through his comedy series Workaholics and Adam DeVine’s House Party. He is also notable for his role in movies such as Pitch Perfect, Neighbors, and The Final Girls, among others.

Just like the romance of other Hollywood stars, the duo met each other while playing supporting roles in the 2014 horror film, The Final Girls and began dating shortly after. They were first spotted during the wrap party of Pretty Little Liars in November 2015. After a few months, the couple confirmed their relationship as they shared pictures of them vacationing in Disneyland. Since then, the couple has continued to dominate the limelight. They often share pictures of each other on Instagram and they also support each other in their respective career.

Since Chloe and Adam have become our favorite celebrity couple, many of their fans have been waiting to hear when the couple will be making their way down the aisle; nevertheless, they appear not to be in a haste. However, during their trip to Paris in 2017, rumors began to fly around that Adam was going to propose to Chloe. In reaction, Adam said that he knows Chloe was expecting that but he was too dumb to understand it at that time. Notwithstanding, he said would propose to her when the time is right. Currently, they have set their eyes on reaching the top of their careers.
