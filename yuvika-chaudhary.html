Yuvika Chaudhary is an actress who was born on 2nd August 1983, hailing from India and exclusively works in the Bollywood film industry. She has acted in various serials and movies including Om Shanti Om starring Shahrukh Khan, Arjun Rampal, and Deepika Padukone. It was the super hit movie and for amazing performances, it was critically acclaimed. Also, the talented actress appeared in some TV reality shows including Bigg Boss 9 and others. Moreover, she has acted in various Indian TV serials for which she got immense love and appreciation from fans and viewers.

Career

The high-spirited and vibrant actress, Yuvika Chaudhary has established her career very broad and worked as an actress not just in Hindi movies, yet in Kannada movies as well. She is mainly known for the role she played in the most-watched movie “Om Shanti Om” starring Shahrukh Khan, Arjun Rampal, and Deepika Padukone. While she also marked an appearance in the movie “Summer” and “Toh Baat Pakki”. In 2009, she also made an appearance in a Kannada movie “Maleyali Jotheyali, in which she appeared in a lead role and worked alongside Ganesh. In 2015, she appeared in the TV reality show “Bigg Boss 9” which was aired on Colors TV. In 2019, she also appeared in the dance reality TV show “Nach Baliye” where she won the dance competition. In 2004, she also took part in Zee Cine Stars Ki Khoj, and afterward, she led to an acting gig for popular serial “Astitva- Ek Prem Kahani”. In 2006, Yuvika also appeared in a music video “Wada Tainu” by Himesh Reshammiya. It was taken from his album “Aap Ka Suroor”. Meanwhile, she also made an appearance in a Coca –Cola advertisement. Moreover, she also worked in the Punjabi movie named “Yaaran Da Katchup” which was released in the year 2014.
