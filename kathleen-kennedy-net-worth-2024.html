Early Life and Career Beginnings

Kathleen Kennedy was born on June 5, 1953, in Berkeley, California. She graduated from San Diego State University with a degree in film and television. Her career in the film industry began as an assistant to John Milius, and later she worked with Steven Spielberg on the film “1941”.
