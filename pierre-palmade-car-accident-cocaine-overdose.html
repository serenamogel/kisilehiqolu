French actor Pierre Palmade car accident injured five, three of them in a critical situation. The accident, compounded with cocaine usage, has left the actor with a serious problem. Is he still in the hospital? Here is an update about the situation.

Pierre Palmade is a French actor and comedian famous for acting in several movies and television shows.

He was born in Bordeaux on March 23, 1968, and grew up with his two sisters.

Palmade’s mother worked as an English teacher, and his father was a doctor who lost his life in a traffic accident when he was eight.

The French actor was previously married to singer Veronique Samson. Their marriage lasted from 1995 to 2001.

Following the divorce, rumors began circulating that Palmade was gay, due to which his marriage ended.

Years later, Palmade came out as gay and confirmed it in an interview in Tetu magazine. 

He said he had relationships with several women but is looking for a serious relationship with a man.

Pierre Palmade Car Accident

A traffic collision that occurred last Friday in Seine-et-Marne has garnered media attention, as actor Pierre Palmade was involved in the crash.

The accident occurred on February 10, 2023, at around 7 p.m. on the RD372 in the Villiers-en-Bière town in Seine-et-Marne, near Dammarie-les-Lys.

Pierre Palmade’s car accident left five injured, three in severe condition. The circumstances of the collision were not specified.

The French actor reportedly hit another car (a Renault Mégane), traveling on the other lane head-on with his car (a Peugeot 3008).

Moments later, the vehicles were hit by another car driven by an old man.

The Renault carried a pregnant woman in her thirties, her six-year-old son, her brother-in-law in his forties, and the vehicle driver.

The actor was taken to the Kremlin-Bicêtre’s hospital and was in critical condition but is currently out of danger.

The pregnant woman was airlifted to the Beaujon hospital in Clichy, and the child was taken to the Necker Hospital.

The driver of the third vehicle was transported to the South Ile-de-France hospital in Melun.

The woman, seven months pregnant, suffered a miscarriage due to the trauma caused by the crash.

It was later announced that the woman was out of danger, but the other three were in critical condition.

Two men in Pierre’s car were seen fleeing the scene as soon as the accident occurred. Their identities have not been uncovered.

Pierre Palmade Cocaine Overdose Possibly Caused The Accident

An initial investigation after the crash concluded that a cocaine overdose possibly caused French actor Pierre Palmade’s car accident.

Toxicology reports showed that Palmade was driving under the influence of cocaine.

The drugs and fear of getting arrested may be why the other two passengers in his cars fled the scene.

As soon as his condition improves, Palmade is set to be tried for possible manslaughter.

According to French law, a driver with narcotics in his system can be charged with up to two years in jail. 

Since Palmade tested positive for cocaine and injured other people, he is expected to face a prison sentence.

The 55 years old was previously charged with drug usage several times. 

In 1995, he was convicted of cocaine usage and subsequently fined 20000 francs.

In 2019, Pierre publicly admitted that he suffered from addiction, saying that coke had ruined his life since his early 20s.
