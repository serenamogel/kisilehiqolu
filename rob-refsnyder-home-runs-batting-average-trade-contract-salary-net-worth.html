Who Is Rob Refsnyder?

Rob is a Korean American baseball player.

He plays as second baseman and right fielder for the Boston Red Sox of the MLB and had previously played for the New York Yankees, Toronto Blue Jays, Tampa Bay Rays, Texas Rangers and Minnesota Twins.

He attended Laguna Hills High School, and the University of Arizona.

As a student at the U of A, he played college baseball for the Arizona Wildcats as a right fielder.

In 2012, he won the College World Series (CWS) as the Most Outstanding Player.

In the same year, Rob was drafted by the Yankees in the fifth round of the MLB draft. He became a right fielder and was top on the list of the team’s prospects.

In 2015, he made his MLB debut and was later traded to the Blue Jays in 2017.

In 2018, he played for the Rays and then the Rangers in 2020. In 2021, he played for the Twins and was signed in with the Red Sox in 2022.
