Watermelon Lemonade Ingredients

There is not a whole lot of ingredients when it comes to homemade lemonade. You’ll see there a just a few additions to make this the best tasting lemonade ever. Let’s take a look at what you’ll need and, as always, you can find the full printable recipe at the end of this post:

  • seedless watermelon – cut into 1-2 inch cubes. If you can’t find seedless watermelon, that’s okay, just make sure you get rid of the seeds before you start.
  • granulated sugar – to sweeten the lemonade.
  • lemon juice – from about 4 large lemons.
  • lime juice – balances the flavor and goes so well with watermelon. You’ll need just 1 large lime.
  • agave nectar or honey – optional if you want it a little sweeter.

Optional garnishes: fresh mint leaves, watermelon slices, lemon and/or lime slices.

How To Make Watermelon Lemonade

This easy lemonade recipe takes about 5 minutes to make! You can find the complete recipe with instructions below. Let’s take a quick look at how to make it:

Instead of making a simple syrup for this lemonade, we’re going to blend the sugar with the watermelon to break it down.

  • Add the cubed watermelon, water, and granulated sugar into a blender. Blend until very smooth. 
  • Pour the watermelon juice through a strainer into a large pitcher or bowl.
  • Stir in the lemon juice, lime juice, and agave (if using) into the pitcher.
  • Pour into glass cups and serve with a slice of watermelon and fresh mint.

    How do I store this Watermelon Lemonade?

    You can store this lemonade in a covered pitcher in the refrigerator. When ready to drink, stir it up with a long spoon as it will have separated. . 

    Can I make limeade instead?

    You sure can. Just swap the amount of lemon juice and lime juice and make sure to test for sweetness. You can always add additional simple syrup, sugar, or agave.

    Can this be frozen?

    Freezing this lemonade is a great idea but we prefer to make it into popsicles!

    Can this be made into a cocktail?

    Sure! This lemonade recipe can turning into a boozy cocktail with the addition of vodka, triple sec, or light rum. Start with 1/2 cup and go from there.

    Variations To Try

    Make this recipe as written once and then go crazy! Here are some fun ideas to add a twist to this lemonade:

    • Add in a lemon-lime soda or ginger ale for an extra sweet kick and some carbonation. 
    • To make this lemonade adult friendly, consider adding in ½- ¾ cup vodka, triple sec, or a light rum. 
    • Smash mint leaves on the bottom of the pitcher for a mint infused watermelon lemonade. 
    • Add additional fruits to your lemonade. Some great fruits to try are blueberries, strawberries, blackberries, peaches, or kiwi.

    Watermelon Lemonade

    This Watermelon Lemonade is the perfect summer drink for BBQs, picnics, poolside parties and more! Easy to make and so incredibly refreshing!

    Course Beverage, Beverages, Drinks Cuisine American Keyword lemonade recipe, watermelon lemonade Prep Time 10 minutes minutes Cook Time 0 minutes minutes Servings 6 Calories 108kcal Author Trish – Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 5 to 6 cups seedless watermelon cut into 1-2 inch cubes
    • 2 cups ice cold water
    • ⅓ cup granulated sugar
    • ½ cup lemon juice about 4 large lemons
    • 2 tablespoons lime juice about 1 large lime
    • 2 tablespoons agave nectar optional, honey or maple syrup can also be used
    • fresh mint leaves, watermelon slices, lemon or lime slices optional garnishes

    Instructions

    • Add the watermelon, water, and granulated sugar into a blender. Blend until very smooth. (I used the smoothie setting twice.)

    • Pour the watermelon juice through a strainer into a large pitcher or bowl. Use a spoon to help push the juice through the strainer as needed.

    • Stir in the lemon juice, lime juice, and agave (if using )into the pitcher and combine until the agave dissolves. Test for sweetness and add additional sugar, agave or honey as needed.

    • Serve over ice with a slice of watermelon and fresh mint.
