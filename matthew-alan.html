Matthew Alan Married Fellow Actress Camilla Luddington in 2019

April 22, 2024
blog

Matthew Alan is an American actor and author who is best known for his recurring role Seth Massey in the Netflix series 13 Reasons Why from 2017 to 2019. In addition to that, he is well known for portraying roles in hits series including as Havemeyer in Snowfall (2017), and as Chris Merrill in the Hulu series Castle Rock.

He was born in 1979 in Evansville, Indiana, the United States as Matthew Gerbig. His dad, Karen Gerbig work as a businessman and mother, Larry Gerbig is a homemaker. Further, he has one brother Chris Gerbig. He completed his graduation from Reitz High School in 1996 and got his degree from Western Kentucky University in 2000.

Who is Matthew Alan's Wife?

She is none other than Camilla Luddington, born on 15 December 1983. She is a British-American actress who is famous for her character Jo Karev in the ABC medical drama, Grey's Anatomy.

Alan met his girlfriend Luddingtonwhile working in a Los Angeles's restaurant in the late 2010s and became stronger ever since. Their first date was at the Temper Trap concert in 2008. After a few years of love romance, they became engaged on January 17, 2018.

The actress, 36, announced their engagement news through her Instagram post by sparking her diamond ring. "So this happened on New Year’s Eve,” she wrote. “I’m not sure I even let Matt get through his proposal before I started yelling YES YES YES!! Of course, it was a yes!”, she added.

The California Outdoor Wedding Ceremony

Finally, the beautiful lovebirds walked down the aisle on 17 August 2019 Saturday. The intimate oceanside ceremony took place at Calamigos Ranch in Malibu, California. It was officiated by their best friend Shawn Aly.

Their reception party was held at the Ritz-Carlton Bacara Hotel in Santa Barbara, California. More than 70 guests including their closest family and friends including former Grey’s costar Jessica Capshaw were attended their day. 

More specially, the duo's 2-year-old daughter Hayden also attended her parents' nuptials. The bride Camilla donned in a beautiful couture white gown with handmade embroidery.

Doting Father Of Two-Hayden & Lucas

Yes, the 5 Feet 9 Inches tall star is a dad of two kiddos he shares with his spouse, Camilla. Before their wedding, they announced on 21 October 2016 that the couple were welcoming their first child together.

On March 9, 2017, their daughter Hayden Alan was born. After seven months of their wedlock, they again announced Luddington's second pregnancy on 9 March 2020. 

Their youngest son Lucas Matthew Alan was born on 7 August 2020 during a pandemic. "Matt and I are SOOO happy to announce the birth of our sweet baby BOY Lucas", his actress wife Camilla wrote on her IG wall.
