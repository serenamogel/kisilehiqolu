The Halloween Baking Championship on Food Network is hosted by comic John Henson. He has nearly 20 years of experience working in the entertainment industry as an actor, presenter, director, and writer.

Henson studied performing in New York City at Boston University, Circle in the Square, Playwrights Horizons, and Three of Us Studios after being born in Stamford, Connecticut, in 1967.

The television personality started acting at the age of eight, and he also dabbled in music and dancing. Because of the white birthmark on the right side of his skull, he was given the names Skunk Boy and Johnny Boy.

He began improv acting while a student at Boston University. At the age of 20, he left school to seek a full-time career as a stand-up comedian.

Before becoming well-known to viewers and reviewers as the host, writer, and co-executive producer of E! Entertainment’s Talk Soup, he began touring the country. According to his official Network biography, John gained a cult following and was nominated for three Emmy Awards.
